Zoom-specific setup and usage guide

What CallCost Does

CallCost is a Zoom app that estimates the live cost of a meeting based on user-entered salary data. It is designed to run inside the Zoom meeting interface.

How to Add the App

  1. Open the CallCost app listing in Zoom App Marketplace.
  2. Click Add to install the app to your Zoom account.
  3. Complete the authorization flow if Zoom asks for confirmation.

How to Open the App in Zoom

  1. Open the Zoom desktop client and join or start a meeting.
  2. Click Apps in the Zoom meeting toolbar.
  3. Search for CallCost and open it.

How to Use the App

  1. Enter your salary and working day settings.
  2. Enter the meeting view and start the timer.
  3. Use Start, Pause, and Reset to control the counter.
  4. Review the estimated meeting cost and your own contribution inside the Zoom app panel.

How to Remove the App

  1. Open Zoom App Marketplace.
  2. Open the CallCost app listing.
  3. Choose the installed app options and remove the app from your account.
