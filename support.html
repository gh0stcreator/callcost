How to Get Help

For support requests, bug reports, or feedback, please use the GitHub repository issues page:

https://github.com/gh0stcreator/callcost/issues

What to Include

  • A short description of the problem.
  • What you expected to happen.
  • What actually happened.
  • Your Zoom client type and, if relevant, screenshots.

Common Topics

  • App not appearing inside Zoom Apps.
  • Meeting cost counter not updating as expected.
  • Questions about local salary storage on the device.
  • General privacy or terms questions.
